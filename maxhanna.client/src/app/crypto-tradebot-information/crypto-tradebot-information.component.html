Overview

The KrakenService TradeBot is an automated trading system designed to execute intelligent crypto/USDC trades on the Kraken exchange based on market conditions and user-defined parameters. It supports three distinct strategies: Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA), Indicator-based (IND), and High-Frequency Trading (HFT).

Available Strategies

1. DCA (Dollar-Cost Averaging)

A steady, disciplined investment approach that makes regular purchases at set intervals, regardless of market conditions.

  • Threshold-based trading: Executes trades when price movements exceed your configured threshold (default: 0.7%)
  • Spread calculation: Compares current price to last trade price to determine trade direction
  • Balanced portfolio: Maintains target allocations between crypto and USDC
  • Premium trade detection: Identifies optimal trading windows for enhanced returns

2. IND (Indicator-Based)

A dynamic strategy that uses technical market indicators to decide when to buy or sell.

  • Technical indicators: Uses RSI, MACD, Moving Averages and other indicators
  • Signal generation: Trades based on indicator crossovers and divergences
  • Flexible timeframes: Apply indicators across different time periods
  • Confirmation filters: Requires multiple indicators to agree before executing

3. HFT (High-Frequency Trading)

Our most aggressive strategy that capitalizes on rapid price movements.

  • Quick execution: Buys as price rises and sells as it falls
  • Tight thresholds: Executes on smaller price movements
  • Higher frequency: More trades with smaller amounts
  • Volume-based: Monitors trading volume for entry/exit signals

Shared Features

Premium Trade Detection (DCA)

The bot identifies optimal trading windows using:

  • Price peak analysis: Detects local maxima in crypto price
  • Volume trends: Identifies declining volume patterns after peaks
  • Timing windows: Only trades within 30-90 minutes after detected peaks
  • Premium bonuses: Increases trade sizes by 5% during premium windows

Safety Mechanisms

  • Price discrepancy checks: Aborts trades if price differences exceed 10%
  • Cooldown periods: 15-minute minimum between trades (varies by strategy)
  • Reserve requirements: Maintains minimum balances
  • Portfolio balance limits: Prevents >90% allocation to either asset

How It Works

Trading Logic Flow

System Checks - Verifies tradebot is active, checks cooldown timer, validates system data is current
Market Analysis - Gets latest price and compares to last trade, calculates price spread percentage, checks for premium trading conditions (DCA)
Trade Execution - For positive spreads (price up): Sells crypto for USDC. For negative spreads (price down): Buys crypto with USDC. Adjusts trade sizes during premium windows.
Post-Trade - Records trade in history, updates wallet balances, begins cooldown timer

Strategy Selection

Choose your strategy based on your investment goals:

  • DCA: Best for long-term investors who want to mitigate market volatility
  • IND: Best for traders who prefer to react to market signals
  • HFT: Best for aggressive traders seeking quick returns

Getting Started

  1. API Setup
    • Provide your Kraken API keys with trading permissions
    • Ensure proper permissions are set (query funds, create orders)
  2. Choose Your Strategy
    • Select DCA for steady, long-term investing
    • Select IND for indicator-driven trading
    • Select HFT for aggressive, frequent trades
  3. Initial Configuration
    • Set your preferred trade parameters
    • Define safety limits appropriate for your portfolio size
  4. First Run
    • Bot will equalize funds if starting from scratch
    • Initial trades may be larger to establish position
  5. Monitoring
    • Review trade history regularly
    • Adjust parameters as needed based on performance

Best Practices

Start Small

  • Begin with smaller trade percentages to test the bot
  • Gradually increase as you gain confidence

Choose the Right Strategy

  • DCA is ideal for long-term holding
  • IND works best with volatile markets
  • HFT requires close monitoring

Monitor Performance

  • Review weekly trade summaries
  • Watch for consistent premium window detection

Adjust for Volatility

  • Consider reducing trade sizes during high volatility
  • Increase thresholds if getting too many trades

Maintain Reserves

  • Ensure minimum reserves are adequate for your portfolio size
  • Replenish funds if balances get too low

Troubleshooting

Common Issues:

  • API Errors: Verify key permissions and connectivity
  • No Trades: Check if price movements exceed your threshold
  • Small Balances: Ensure you have sufficient funds above minimums
  • Frequent Trades: Adjust cooldown timer or increase threshold

For additional support, consult the trade logs which detail every decision the bot makes.
